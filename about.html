About Me

My name is Nikolay Arhipov and I am a software engineer from Latvia.

I am passionate about programming. I find it important to not simply develop and ship a product which meets clients requirements, but to make something that exceed. I believe that the result of my job should be something that improves users experience from any perspective.

Since I am a technical person, it would be unnatural not to mention the enthusiasm, with which I always look forward towards learning new technologies, approaches and methods that could influence, inspire and potentially improve the result of my work. At the same time I find it even more important to understand how the things that I am used to work. Since there is no such thing as dated algorithms, data structures, or even mathematical models, in my opinion there is no reason jumping on trending technologies, if you did not thoroughly study the existing solutions for the problem that you already use or might have used.
